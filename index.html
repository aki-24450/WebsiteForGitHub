But first,where is Tonga located?

According to Wikipedia, Tonga is one of the smallest island located in the South Pacific Ocean.It is the the only perfect island to not be colonised.It has 169 islands and 36 of them are being inhabited,which are in 3 main groups Vava'u,Ha'apai and Tongatapu. Due to the spread out islands it has the 40th largest Exclusive Economic Zone of 659,558 km2 (254,657 sq mi).
